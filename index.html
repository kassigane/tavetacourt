Our Services

Embracing Automation, Enhancing Justice

E-Filing

Taveta Law Courts has gone digital and is championing e-filing. Electronic filing (e-filing) is the creation and submission of case documents electronically to Court Registries. This web-based application can be accessed from any location with an internet connection, allowing law firms, lawyers, and other litigants to initiate and complete the process of filing cases online from their offices or remotely. With e-filing, there is no need to physically visit courts to file cases or banking halls to pay court fees.

Public Kiosk

Taveta Law Courts is harnessing the power of digital innovation with the introduction of the Public Information Kiosk. This self-service portal offers a convenient and efficient way for users to access vital case information. By using the kiosk, individuals can easily search for their case details, track case activities, and obtain information on court fees. This user-friendly system ensures that the public can stay informed about their legal matters without the need to visit the courthouse in person.

Mahakama Popote

Launched in October 2022, the Mahakama Popote initiative harnesses the power of ICT to allow judicial officers in less busy stations to hear cases from courts with higher caseloads virtually. Initially piloted in Milimani Commercial Court and Law Courts, Mahakama Popote has significantly boosted judicial efficiency and reach. Taveta Law Courts proudly champions this initiative, hearing matters from Mombasa Law Courts and aiding in the decongestion efforts.

Causelist Portal

The Judiciary is advancing its digital services with the introduction of the Causelist Portal. This online platform allows litigants, lawyers, and the general public to easily access and download daily and weekly causelists. By providing up-to-date scheduling information, the Causelist Portal ensures that all parties are well-informed about court proceedings. This innovative service not only enhances convenience but also improves the efficiency and transparency of the judicial process at Taveta Law Courts.
